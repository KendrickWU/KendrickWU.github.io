**Status:** Manuscript in preparation for *Operations Research*<br>
**Context:** Collaboration with a real EV charging operator serving European customers

This work studies the joint control of pricing or admission and physical power allocation in capacity-constrained EV charging operations.

The central question is when commercial decisions can be separated from charging schedules and when congestion, service quality, and grid constraints require them to be optimized jointly. The project extends the service-side charging model toward stochastic scheduling, fluid approximations, and implementable control policies.

Field requirements and charging-session structure from the industry collaboration guide the model and evaluation design; claims are restricted to what the available operational data can support.
