**Course Description**: Statistics for Financial Engineering covers fundamental statistical methods and their applications in financial analysis, including exploratory data analysis, resampling methods, multivariate analysis, and financial modeling.

**Teaching Responsibilities**:

- Led tutorial sessions and office hours
- Prepared and graded homework assignments (10 homework sets)
- Developed homework solutions and instruction slides
- Assisted with midterm and final exam preparation and grading
- Topics covered: EDA, resampling/bootstrap methods, multivariate analysis, copulas, time series analysis, fixed income securities
